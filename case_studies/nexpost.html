NexPost's Inspired Post Generation

NexPost.ai is an AI-powered social media content assistant that helps you create content posts across platforms like LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Reddit, and blogs. It leverages LLMs to generate social media posts content such as text and images. My friend Nia made it and it is great so check it out!

Overview

Content creators wishes to post content on their social media accounts in order to generate reach and attract a bigger audience. However, they may not always have the time or the creativity to come up with new content. NexPost.ai's Inspired Post Generation feature allows users to generate new posts based on existing content, making it easier for them to share relevant and engaging content with their audience.

Goal

We are building a feature to make it easier for users to generate posts so the main goal here is to drive higher activation and retention of NexPost.ai.

Constraints

Unlike other features of content generation in NexPost.ai where the content ideas stems from the user, this one is sourced from another author. This means it is difficult to assign responsibility for inappropriate content. NexPost.ai must flag a warning if the generated content may be deemed inappropriate or offensive.

Customer segmentations

There are several types of content creators that we could possibly target:
  • Personal Brand Builders
    These are the users that want to build up their following in order to become influencers in their respective fields. They may be looking to build up their personal brand in order to attract job offers, consulting gigs, or speaking engagements.
  • Business Promoters
    These are the users that want to promote their business or products. They may be looking to drive traffic to their website or online store in order to generate sales or other platforms such as YouTube to generate ad revenue.
  • Thought Leaders
    These are the users that want to share their expertise and insights with their audience. They may be looking to establish themselves as thought leaders in their respective fields in order to attract consulting gigs, speaking engagements, or book deals.
Based on our goal of driving higher adoption and engagement amongst existing users, we would target the Personal Brand Builders. The reason for this is because they are frequently (low stakes, high volume) with types of content such as quick top of the mind thoughts or personal anecdotes. Comparatively, both the Business Promoters and Thought Leaders have more indepth content with higher stakes.

Use Cases and Pain Points

The user comes across a post they really like and wishes to repost it. However, they want to either:
  • Copy/Paste but Different
    The user wishes to, as quickly and as easily as possible, repost this to their audience but they do not want to be seen as somebody who copies other people's content without giving credit.
    • Pain Point
      The user has to invest non-insignificant mental effort to think about how to change the content. This is especially true if the user is not a native speaker of the language of the post.
  • Content Modification
    In this case, the user wishes to either change the content slightly to make it more relevant to their audience and/or they may wish to change the style of the message to fit their writing style.
    • Pain Point
      The user has to invest non-insignificant mental effort to think about how to change the content. This is especially true if the user is not a native speaker of the language of the post.

Prioritized Solutions

Given the use cases and pain points, the solutions in a priority order are:
  • Copy/Paste but Different
    NexPost.ai will generate a new post that conveys the same meaning but using slightly different words, different ordering, or presentation style (perhaps where the content is in an ordered list to the content being prefixed by an emoji). If there is a critical point that is saved for last to keep the reader engaged (such as "read until the end for the best tip"), then that ordering will be preserved. Any emojis are subjected to be switched out and/or reordered as long as the meaning is preserved.
    • Priority 1
      The reason this is first is because this is the lowest effort scenario for the user. All they need to do is copy and paste the content.
  • Content Modification
    In addition to the various customization (Writing Tone, Creativity Slider, Post Size slider, etc), NexPost.ai will allow the user to enter two pieces of information: The original post and ther desired modification. NexPost.ai will detect whether the two are in conflict with each other and will prompt the user to resolve the conflict. An example of this would be if the original content talks about french fries best dipped with tartar sauce while the added modification talks about the best dip for french fries being ketchup. In this case, the user will be prompted to either choose one or the other or to rephrase the modification to be more in line with the original content. After conflict resolution is complete, NexPost.ai will generate a new post that conveys the same meaning as the original post but with the desired modification.
    • Priority 2
      The reason this is second is because this step requires a bit more effort from the user and we want to offer the lowest barrier of entry solution first. This can be viewed as a feature-add later.
All solutions will subject the input data for inappropriate content detection and will flag a warning if the generated content may be deemed inappropriate or offensive either globally or to a specific region or audience.

Dependencies

In a rarity, there really is not much that the MVP of this product is dependent on besides the LLM it needs to do the generation. It relies on the user to copy/paste the material so there are no APIs it needs to rely on being supported. Besides the usual infrastructure staying alive, it is self-contained.

Risks and Mitigation

  • Inconsistent settings and message
    Given there are custom settings such as Writing Tone where it can range from Professional or Helpful to Humorous or Persuasive, the user may accidentally set the wrong tone for the generated content, especially if the user did not read it enough or it is written in a language they are not proficient in.
    • Mitigation
      While the user is ultimately responsible for the generated content, one thing that we can do is ask in the prompt to detect the writing tone of the original content and defaulty select that tone for the newly generated content. Before generation, we can still offer a way for the user to change the tone if they wish so we do not rip control from them.
  • Detection of sarcasm
    Considering the expected low bar of effort from the user or possibly the language the original content was written in may not be one of the user's proficient languages, the user may not be able to detect sarcasm in the original content. Currently, LLMs are not consistent in detecting sarcasm. This may lead to generated content that puts the user in a poor light.
    • Mitigation
      We can try to have the prompt edited so that it tries to detect sarcasm but this may not work given the current performance of the LLM. In addition to the attempt at sarcasm detection, we can ask the LLM given the user's historical post and social media content, is what was generated consistent with the user's message or beneficial to their cause.

Metrics for Success

  • Primary Metrics for Activation and Retention
    • Daily Active Users
      We are expecting more users to use this as it is lowest cost of entry.
      • For the purposes of this study, we can set this to be 5% growth rate per month.
    • Day over Day Retention rate
      We want to measure this day over day as we expect users to be posting on social media 7 days of the week.
      • For the purposes of this case study, we can set this target to be > 30%. We can adjust this as we start seeing more data.
  • Secondary Metrics
    • Usage Frequency
      This will help us know if we even have an audience or not. We should also put, as a background, the daily active users of the game in general.
      • For the purposes of this case study, we can set this target to be > 1 per day. 1 per day seems to fit a nice quota to stay on people's radar but this is something we should certainly look at in the future, especially when compared to usage of the other generation types.
    • Frequency of use for the same source material
      We would want this to be as low as possible as that would indicate the user is satisfied with the first generation and does not need to keep going back to the same source material.
      • For the purposes of this case study, we can set this to be < 3 times at the 50th percentile mark.
    • Week over Week Retention rate
      This will let us know if users, after they have had time to digest their results and how useful the tool is, comes back for more.
      • For the purposes of this case study, we can set this to be > 75%.

Future Roadmap

  • Content parsing from link
    Instead of having the user copy and paste the content, they can share a link to the post. NexPost.ai will then extract the content and generate an inspired post based on that.
  • Content generation from image and video
    Instead of just being able to generate content from text, NexPost.ai can also generate content from images and videos. This is especially useful for platforms like Instagram where the image is the main content and YouTube where the videos is the main content.
  • Content based on a topic
    Instead of generating a post based on another post but also not the current method of topic + thoughts from the author, NexPost allows creation of a content based on topic alone. This is especially useful for users that may not have a specific post in mind but have a general idea of what they want to post about.
  • Unprompted Topic Generation
    Instead of the user discovering a topic to speak about, NexPost.ai goes and searches online the most talked about topics in either the user's area of expertise, what resonates with their existing audience, or what is trending with an audience group they wish to capture.
  • Top post gatherer
    Upon given a topic, Nexpost.ai will scour the internet and gather the highest trending posts about that topic. The user can then select one of these posts to be the source of inspiration for their new post.
